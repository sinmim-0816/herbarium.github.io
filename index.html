The Art of Preservation

Turning Nature Into Timeless Specimens




Herbarium

A herbarium is a unique collection of preserved plant specimens, meticulously cataloged for scientific research. These collections help us understand plant diversity, track ecological changes, and study evolution over time. By documenting plants from various regions and periods, herbaria provide crucial insights into biodiversity and environmental shifts.


Read More ⟶

Role of Herbarium

  • Herbaria aid in taxonomy, systematics, and conservation biology.
  • They support research in medicine, agriculture, and industry.
  • Herbaria provide plant specimens for identification and comparison.
  • They record plant distribution, helping track environmental changes over time.
